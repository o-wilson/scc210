This game requires use of the keyboard and mouse.


Use the mouse to select options in the menu.

To control the player use a keyboard:
    W to go up
    A to go left
    S to go down
    D to go right
    Space to use the slam move

The goal of the game is to avoid obstacles for as long as possible. You do this by moving your 
player car around the road to avoid the obstacles.

You can use the slam move once every three seconds. It removes any obstacles that you move into
during its duration without you taking damage.

You have 5 health. You lose health by running into obstacles. When you run out of health, the game
is over.

Fuel goes down over time, pick up fuel to refill it. When you run out, the game is over.
